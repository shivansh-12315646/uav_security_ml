Evaluation Metrics Reference
Silhouette Score

Measures intra-cluster cohesion vs inter-cluster separation. Range: [-1, 1]. Higher = better.

Calinski-Harabasz

Between-cluster to within-cluster variance ratio. Higher = denser, well-separated clusters.

Davies-Bouldin

Average cluster similarity. Lower = better. Perfect clustering = 0.

Anomaly Rate

Isolation Forest contamination. Negative decision scores indicate anomalous UAV behavior.
